# Google Build with AI

Major League Hacking (MLH) and Google have teamed up to help you host a Build with AI Hack Days. Google has tons of awesome AI-powered tools for hackers to leverage during your event. We encourage hackers to use Google AI Studio, Google Antigravity, Gemini CLI, and the Google Gemini APIs to build an awesome back, then deploy it on Google Cloud.&#x20;

As an organizer, you'll have the opportunity to attend a workshop with the MLH and Google teams where you'll learn how to host an amazing Hack Day:

* **Organizing 101:** How to run a successful event without the burnout.
* **Google AI Stack:** Quick-start demos of Google AI Studio, Gemini CLI, Antigravity and deploying apps to Google Cloud.
* **Funding & Credits:** Exactly how to claim your event's Cloud credits and food budget.
* **Priority Access:** How to secure one of the 50 limited spots in the Build with AI Hack Days

RSVP to either of the workshop sessions here!

As part of hosting a Hack Day, you'll run a "Best Use of Google Gemini" and "Best Hack Deployed on Google Cloud" prize category. Here are descriptions for each category. You should add these to your event's Devpost page.&#x20;

### Best Use of Gemini API

It’s time to push the boundaries of what's possible with AI using Google Gemini. Check out the Gemini API to build AI-powered apps that make your friends say WHOA. So, what can Gemini do for your hackathon project?

* Understand language like a human and build a chatbot that gives personalized advice
* Analyze info like a supercomputer and create an app that summarizes complex research papers
* Generate creative content like code, scripts, music, and more

Think of the possibilities… what will you build with the Google Gemini API this weekend?

### Best App Deployed on Google Cloud

Ready to take your project from "it works on my machine" to "it works for the world"? It’s time to harness the same infrastructure that powers Google. Whether you’re building a viral social app or a tool to solve global challenges, Google Cloud gives you the scale, speed, and reliability to launch with confidence.

The sky isn't the limit. The Cloud is. What will you deploy on Google Cloud this weekend?

### Slides

Upon event approval, you will receive a set of slides as part of the official onboarding packet. You should present these slides to participants as part of your opening ceremony.

### Judging Info

The most important thing is to validate they're actually using the Gemini API in their app and / or have fully deployed the app to Google Cloud.&#x20;
