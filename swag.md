# Swag

We will be sending you an event pack full of swag for your event! We include swag based on the expected number of attendees at your event (accounting for some attrition from sign-ups).

You should expect to receive the following items:

* [ ] MLH Logo Stickers
* [ ] MLH Season Stickers
* [ ] Hack Day I Demoed Stickers (Give these out to hackers who show off their projects!)
* [ ] Prizes for your top team
  * [ ] In some cases, we may award digital gift cards to your winning team post-event instead.
* [ ] Swag from MLH's Partners
